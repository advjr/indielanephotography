Meet Donna

A calm, personable approach that puts you at ease

Most people tell me they hate being in front of a camera. That is exactly where I love to start. I take the time to get to know you and your vision, then create a relaxed space where you can be yourself and have a little fun.

The result is honest, vibrant images that actually look like you. After years on the Surf Coast in Victoria, I have moved to the Byron Bay region and I now photograph clients across Northern NSW, Tweed Heads and the Gold Coast, with regular trips back to Victoria.
